ABOUT US

First-ever environment friendly total laundry solution in Kerala to cater to all your laundry woes. Amidst hustle-bustle of unorganized and unreliable laundry services iWash brings you an affordable, clean, and quick service solution, which is designed keeping in mind you and your needs.

iWash is a young startup, which incubated in a dormitory. As students we always faced time crunch and the biggest task was to wash & get clean and stiff clothes. We wondered if ever there was an outlet, which could provide total solution. After realizing the need and importance of this service, we came up with a total solution that will cater to the needs of people in and around hamlets of Kollam.

Our state-of-the-art technology and cleansing agent can handle with care different fabrics, ensuring clean wash and protected linens.
