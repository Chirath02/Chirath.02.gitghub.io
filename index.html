HOW WE WORK

Collection Of Clothes

The clothes given at each collection center will be delivered to iwash laundry

Sorting And Tagging

The clothes given will be sorted and tagged as per the choice of the customers.

Washing

The clothes are washed in hi-tech Ramsons Washing machine that could wash upto 100 clothes per wash. 10% water is removed in the washing machine itself.

EXTRACTION

The wet clothes are moved to the extractor that can extract upto 80% of the water in the clothes. The clothes are then transfered to the Dryer where the clothes get dry which is further put under sunlight.

IRONING

The clothes then are ironed in our Vaccum Ironing System which press the cloth with steam on its surface and sucks from down due to vaccum.

QUALITY CHECK

The clothes are re-checked if all the conditions of customers are satisfied or not and if not it would re-done else would be further proceeded for billing.

ABOUT US

First-ever environment friendly total laundry solution in Kerala to cater to all your laundry woes. Amidst hustle-bustle of unorganized and unreliable laundry services iWash brings you an affordable, clean, and quick service solution, which is designed keeping in mind you and your needs.

iWash is a young startup, which incubated in a dormitory. As students we always faced time crunch and the biggest task was to wash & get clean and stiff clothes. We wondered if ever there was an outlet, which could provide total solution. After realizing the need and importance of this service, we came up with a total solution that will cater to the needs of people in and around hamlets of Kollam.

Our state-of-the-art technology and cleansing agent can handle with care different fabrics, ensuring clean wash and protected linens.
